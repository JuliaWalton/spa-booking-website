About

It's All About Your Comfort

Welcome to Spa Selene, where we invite you to embark on a journey of rejuvenation and relaxation. Nestled in the heart of Dallas, our spa is a haven of tranquility that offers a respite from the hustle and bustle of everyday life. We have happily been serving our community in the Dallas Metroplex for over 10 years.

At Spa Selene, we believe in the power of self-care and holistic well-being. Our team of experienced and skilled therapists is dedicated to providing you with a transformative spa experience that revitalizes your body, mind, and spirit. From soothing massages to invigorating facials, each treatment is designed to promote balance, harmony, and a sense of renewal.

Whether you're seeking a moment of escape from the demands of daily life, recovering from a strenuous workout, or simply indulging in self-care, Spa Selene is committed to providing you with an unforgettable and revitalizing experience. Step into our oasis of calmness, where relaxation and rejuvenation await you. Embrace the journey to a healthier, happier, and more balanced you at Spa Selene today.

Scheduling an Appointment

To schedule an appointment, please utilize our site to book online. For the ultimate spa experience, we recommend arriving one hour prior to your scheduled service time to relax and unwind in our spa amenities before your treatment.

Cancellation Policy

A 100% charge will be incurred for any treatment not canceled at least 24 hours to your scheduled time. Late arrival will result in a shortened treatment time.

Health Conditions

Kindly advise us of any health conditions, allergies, or injuries which could affect your choice of treatment when making your spa reservation.

Pregnancy

We have specially designed treatments for expectant mothers. Please allow our spa team to guide you in selecting treatments that are suitable during this special time.
