Amenities

Surround Yourself in Serene Style

Our spa environment is carefully curated to create a peaceful and inviting atmosphere. Immerse yourself in the soothing ambiance, where soft lighting, calming scents, and tranquil music create the perfect backdrop for your relaxation journey. Our luxurious facilities include private treatment rooms, a serene lounge area, and state-of-the-art amenities to enhance your overall experience.

At Spa Selene, we prioritize the use of natural and high-quality products in our treatments, ensuring that you receive the utmost care and nourishment for your skin and body. Our therapists are trained in a variety of techniques, allowing us to tailor each session to meet your individual needs and preferences.

What to Expect


State of the Art Facilities

Our spa features modern designs with luxurious finishes. We invite you to utilize our pool, hot tub, and steam sauna, and lounge prior to any of our services.

Medical Grade Products

We take pride in selecting the finest products for our treatments. Select products used are available for purchase in the lobby. 40% discounts on products are offered with the purchase of a service exceeding $300.

Yoga in the Garden

Spa Selene is excited to host Free-Yoga-Friday's in our garden for the members of our spa community. This all-levels yoga flow is taught by our staff member and gifted-yogi, Liz.

Complimentary Beverages & Bites

Guests of Spa Selene are welcome to indulge in a selection of wine, coffee, tea, and healthy food options. Menu's are available upon request.
